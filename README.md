# ⚔️ The Scorned Knight

A Python text-based dungeon crawler built by three friends for a school project. Navigate treacherous rooms, fight enemies, manage your inventory, and face a terrifying boss — all in your terminal.

---

## The Story

You are the Scorned Knight — betrayed, forgotten, and left for dead. Now you rise from the depths of a cursed dungeon, fighting your way back to the surface. Every room holds danger. Every choice matters. Every second counts.

---

## ✨ Features

**⚔️ Combat System**
Turn-based combat where your decisions determine whether you live or die. Choose your attacks wisely.

**🎒 Inventory & Items**
Pick up weapons, potions, and gear as you explore. Manage your inventory carefully — you never know what you'll need next.

**🗺️ Multiple Rooms & Levels**
A sprawling dungeon with multiple rooms and levels to explore, each with its own enemies and secrets.

**📖 Story & Lore**
A full narrative woven through the gameplay — the dungeon has a history, and the Scorned Knight has a score to settle.

**👹 Boss Fights**
Face a powerful boss at the end of the dungeon. Good luck. You'll need it.

**❤️ Character Stats**
Track your HP, attack power, and more. Watch your stats carefully — death is permanent.

**⚡ Quicktime Events**
React fast or face the consequences. Quicktime events add a layer of real-time tension to an otherwise turn-based world.

---

## ▶️ Run It

```bash
python scorned_knight.py
```

No dependencies — pure Python, runs anywhere.
